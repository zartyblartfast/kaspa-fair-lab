Root cause:
The ENV-060C live covenant UTXO was created by a TX_VERSION_TOCCATA/version-1 transaction, but the covenant script was adapted from the official crypto/txscript/examples/covenants.rs version-0 in-VM example. The first script check recomputes TransactionID(prev_tx_rest || prev_tx_payload) and compares it to the input outpoint txid. That works for the upstream v0 example because its stack item is transaction_v0_id_preimage split into rest/payload. It fails for the live v1 create tx because v1 txid is TransactionV1Id(payload_digest || rest_digest), not TransactionID(transaction_v1_rest_preimage || payload).

Exact failing check/opcode:
first OP_EQUALVERIFY in build_covenant_script(), immediately after OP_BLAKE2B_WITH_KEY and OP_OUTPOINT_TX_ID.
